COL Radio

AN INITIATIVE BY OPEN LEARNING DEVELOPMENT CENTRE, UNIVERSITY OF DELHI.

*

COL Radio offers students and learners access to academic and non-academic audio and video content that supports and supplements learning, awareness, and creative expression beyond traditional settings and study materials thereby enhancing and enriching the learning experiences.

01

Our
Vision

To become a trusted digital radio platform that enhances learning, encourages dialogue, and expands access to quality education through engaging and inclusive audio content.

02

Our
Mission

To support academic learning through reliable and engaging audio content aligned with open and distance education, while encouraging inclusive and learner-centric student participation.

03

Our
Goal

To expand access to quality educational content by leveraging digital media platforms for wider reach and meaningful impact.
